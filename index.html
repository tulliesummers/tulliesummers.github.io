About Tullie Summers

Steeped in raw emotion, complex character dynamics and the haunting weight of trauma, healing and redemption, Tullie Summers brings to life the tidal wave contemporary romances can experience. With a passion for hard-won hope and stories of resilience, she writes for readers who want their hearts smashed and pieced back together. Happily married to her high school sweetheart, Tullie Summer’s life has been akin to many of the romance novels we all long for with just enough tragedy and struggle to keep the pages turning. She loves spending her days painting, baking and enjoying picnics, usually with a book in hand. She spins stories of love, loss and never guarantees a happy ending.
